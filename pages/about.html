About

I graduated from the University of New South Wales in Computer Engineering BE. After I graduated, I returned to Hong Kong to start my work career.


In early years, I was being seconded to two companies to gain all the fundamental programming and debugging skills.


Then I joined a telecom company, maintained and supported several digital marketing products.


In later years, joined in-house team, designed and implemented business self-service order email extraction automation flow and ticketing portal for Network team but was not as involved in software development.


Through futureproof, I received 13-week intensive training in web development. I had transformed into a Software Developer and looking forward to the next step in my career.
